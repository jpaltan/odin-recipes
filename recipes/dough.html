The dough chillin' like a mofo.

Ingredients:

  • 300 grams flour
  • 220 grams water
  • 5 grams instant dry yeast
  • 9 grams salt
  • oil as needed

The steps:

  1. In a stand mixer fitted with the dough, combine flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Stir to combine, then add water. Mix on low speed until dough comes together in a rough ball, then shut off the mixer and let rest for ten minutes.
  3. Continue mixing at medium-low speed until the dough forms a smooth, silky ball ,about 10 minutes longer. It should stick to the bottom of the bowl rather than riding around the edges.
  4. Remove dough hook, form dough into a tight ball, set in the bottom of the mixer bowl, cover tightly with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place until the dough has roughly doubled in volume, about 2 hours.
