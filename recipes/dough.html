The Dough is chilled like a mofo.

  • 825 grams flour
  • 500 grams water
  • 2 grams instant dry yeast
  • 16.5 grams salt
  • 12.5 grams oil
    1. Mix flour and water to hydrolozye for 20 minutes
    2. Stir in yeast
    3. Mix flour and water in stand mixer
    4. Slowly incorporate yeast
    5. Mix for a wee bit
    6. Add salt and oil
